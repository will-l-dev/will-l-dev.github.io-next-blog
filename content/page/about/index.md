## Will Louzado

Senior Platform Engineer at [Beyond](https://www.bynd.com/). Self-taught. 5x Google Cloud certified. 3x Dad. Based in Royal Wootton Bassett, England.

## The story

I spent over a decade in tech recruitment — Senior Technical Recruiter, Talent Acquisition Lead, the works. I was good at it. But I always wanted to be on the other side of the table.

In late 2022 I did a software engineering bootcamp. While still working as a Talent Acquisition Lead at Qodea, I started a secondment into the Platform Engineering team — somehow splitting both roles at once (my own CV literally says "somehow!"). It worked. I made the full switch to Platform Engineer in early 2023 and haven't looked back.

I've worked across financial services, public sector, and logistics — migrations to GCP, GKE, AlloyDB, security and observability, infrastructure automation, the lot. Promoted to Senior Platform Engineer in March 2026, two months before landing in Las Vegas.

## Why I'm here

I first heard about Google Cloud Next back in 2021 when I was still a recruiter. I thought it looked like the kind of event I'd love to attend one day. I imagined turning up as a Talent Acquisition specialist, networking, picking up business cards.

Instead I'm here as a Senior Platform Engineer, courtesy of [Beyond](https://www.bynd.com/).

Life has a funny way of working out.
